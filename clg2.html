About Us

Welcome to St. Joseph's First Grade College! Our institution is dedicated to providing top-quality education in computer science and other disciplines, helping students build strong foundations for their future careers.

We focus on fostering innovation, creativity, and critical thinking through our diverse range of programs and extracurricular activities. Join us to explore, learn, and grow in a supportive and dynamic environment.

St. Joseph’s First Grade College is one of the pioneering institutions under MDES (Mysore Diocesan Educational Society) that has been rendering yeomen service in the field of education. With more than five decades of experience in transforming young minds, the institution caters students from nursery to undergraduate courses. St. Joseph’s stands out because of its quality education endured by human values. Innumerable success stories of our alumni around the globe drive our passion to surpass our past best. The Josephite family cordially welcomes you for a memorable journey ahead.
